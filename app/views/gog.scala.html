Welcome to the ‘Game of Games.’ Get ready to play…and learn!

The ‘Game of Games’ is a card game designed to bring the ‘Playing is Learning’ initiative home with you.

(1) Did you know? communicates general concepts about play.

(2) Try this at home describes games you can play at home whenever you and your child have a few minutes.

Collect the Game of Games cards at participating sites. When you and your child are ready to play, shuffle the Game of Games deck. Randomly pick a card and play the game on the card for about 5 minutes. Repeat as many times as time allows.

To get started, go to our registration page. This will give you access to a set of on-line cards. You will also receive a code. Bring this code to the main desk at a participating site to pick up your Passport to Play. Once you have your passport, collect the cards and stamp your passport to get a special introductory gift!
